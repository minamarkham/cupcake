Cleveland Rape Crisis Center is the only local organization providing comprehensive rape crisis intervention and prevention services to survivors and their supporters. Your support enables Cleveland Rape Crisis Center to provide all services completely free of charge, including therapeutic services, the 24-hour crisis hotline, education programs and advocacy services. Over 17,000 people receive our free services every year. If you would like to support the work of the Cleveland Rape Crisis Center, please make a tax-deductible contribution. Your support directly impacts the lives of survivors. Thank you. Here is what your donation will provide: $50 will provide a one-hour art therapy session to a child survivor of rape or sexual abuse $100 will underwrite an education and rape sensitivity workshop for police officers $250 will allow two girls to participate in a day-long empowerment and self esteem conference for at-risk teenage girls $500 will provide 24 hours of Justice System Advocacy to assist a survivor in navigating the legal system $1,000 will provide twenty hours of individual therapy for one adult rape survivor $2,500 will support 40 hours of crisis intervention training for an entire class of volunteers to answer the 24-hour rape crisis hotline. Because of this service, every hour of the day, every day of the year, staff and volunteers of the Cleveland Rape Crisis Center are on call to advocate for the needs of rape survivors and their families.
